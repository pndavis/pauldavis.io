Fall Quarter Update

Posted by Paul on December 30, 2018

Greetings from Seattle!

My first quarter at the University of Washington is done! It's been an absolutely wonderful experience so far, and has been amazing just to see the many ways that God has been at work.

I'm super blessed to be in a great location near campus. It's a quick walk to get to most places and my place has plenty of space to host events. My roommate has also been a huge blessing, and has been super helpful in acclimating to the area. Seattle is a beautiful city, and the University of Washington is no exception. This the Drumheller Fountain on campus with Mt Rainier seen in the background. We had a wonderful fall retreat, the students heard a message from an entrepreneur in our church about the importance of rest as a Christian. During finals week we handed out care packages to students passing by filled with food and notes of encouragement. Our final event of the quarter was a Christmas party hosted by a family in a nearby church.

Thank you so much for your continued prayers and support. It has been such a blessing to see the ways God has been at work, and I'm so excited to see what the rest of the year holds.

Prayer Requests:

  • Pray I would continue to build strong relationships with students
  • Pray that I would continue to adjust to the area and the job
  • Pray I would continue to make new friends in the area
  • Pray for my ongoing financial support
