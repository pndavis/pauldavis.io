Ready to Move!

Posted by Paul on Septemeber 2, 2018

Hey Friends! This summer has been an amazing experience. I have been so blessed by so many people excited about how God continues to be at work in my life. Through your prayers and support I have reached my goal to move!

I will be moving to Seattle this Wednesday, the 6th for to begin this next adventure at the University of Washington.

I had the privilege of speaking in front of my church about what the next year in ministry looks like for me. Then the elders prayed over me as they send me out to Washington.

Prayer Requests:

  • For my move to Seattle and adjustment to the area.
  • To reach the last 5% of my funds to be fully funded for the year.
  • For the incoming students at the University of Washington. Classes start on the 24th.
